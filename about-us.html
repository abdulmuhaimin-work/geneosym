About Us

The name ‘Geneosym’ comes from a combination of three words. 'Genesis' which means beginning, 'neo' which means new or recent, and 'symbiosis' which means collaboration. Just as our name suggests, we will create collaborations between all industries and also bring a fresh breath of air in the industry using technology. Geneosym Tech PLT was established in UPM-MTDC, Serdang, Malaysia in 2017 as a technology-based company.


Mission & Vision

We strive to increase the number of technology-based products in the national economic landscape; revenue and jobs generator for the Malaysian economy prior to increase the pool of young entrepreneurs with excellent business knowledge and skills. We intend to maintain the high quality, reliability and excellent service that have always been our hallmark to our customers.
